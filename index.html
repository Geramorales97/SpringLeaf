Greek Salad

Ingredients
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
How to make

In a large bowl, combine the cucumber, onion, bell pepper, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.

Roasted Vegetable Salad

Ingredients
  • 1 small head cauliflower, chopped into florets
  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled and diced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
How to make

Preheat the oven to 400Â°F. In a large bowl, toss together the cauliflower, butternut squash, onion, and bell pepper with the olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread the vegetables out on a baking sheet and roast for 30-40 minutes, until tender and caramelized. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar and honey. In a large bowl, toss together the roasted vegetables, spinach, goat cheese, and balsamic dressing.

Quinoa and Vegetable Salad

Ingredients
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small zucchini, diced
  • 1 small yellow squash, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
How to make

Rinse the quinoa under cold water and drain. In a medium saucepan, combine the quinoa and water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, until the quinoa is tender and the water has been absorbed. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, bell peppers, zucchini, yellow squash, onion, parsley, and mint. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
